Interactive Poem: When Pixels Cry

●     ●
(  ___  )
 ^^^
Small hands on glass screens,
In this blue-light world.
Your laughter buffering ...
Your dreams corrupted.
Tiny fingers scroll and swipe,
Searching for what love feels like.
But the network cannot reach
The emptiness that pixels teach.

Connection.exe has failed.

This represents the failure of digital technology to provide genuine human connection
