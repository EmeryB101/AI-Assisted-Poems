Connected/Disconnected

AI-Assisted Poems Portfolio

Artist Statement

Anthropic's recent launch of Claude Code in October 2025 is a coding assistant for various individuals, groups, and entities. With the increasing attention of scholars grappling with how the ongoing advancements of AI fit into their field and determining how authorship, credibility, and ethics play a role in the agentic elements of these tools I argue that AI can be a digital companion to creative works. This explores agentic AI as a digital companion to rhetoric and creative scholarship.

I advocate for the human thought to remain central at the forefront of work that is displayed through prompting detailed and strategic messaging and debugging alongside AI. This talk will analyze the portfolio of code I created through Claude Code and my journey through ongoing shifts between the advancements and implications of AI and its capabilities in various ways. My example will explore "Connected/Disconnected." Each poem presented a different type of poem, including visual, erasure, and interactive.

Pushing the boundaries of AI developments, I argue that agentic AI offers digital capabilities that invite participants to the benefits of code and digital media-centered works, which will be seen through interactive elements, and digital design. This will demonstrate various ways to keep human thought at the forefront and demonstrate how specific prompting and understanding of Claude Code impacts the final work.
