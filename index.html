Connected/Disconnected

AI-Assisted Poems Portfolio

Artist Statement

This collection of AI-assisted poems uses Claude Code as a digital companion for interactive and multimodal pieces. My collection "Connected/Disconnected" is meant to mirror the ongoing push and pull of technology and society. The theme of this poem was to connect these juxtaposing ideas by continuously asking Claude Code to make poems as emotional as possible, pushing the boundaries of AI and pathos.

In this portfolio, I have included five varying poems of this theme: two erasure poems (one visual, one source text), two visual poems, and an interactive kinetic poem. Each poem is meant to build upon this narrative from various perspectives and showcase how digital poetry can expand upon genre while also acknowledging implications of AI within creative works. I hope you enjoy my portfolio.
